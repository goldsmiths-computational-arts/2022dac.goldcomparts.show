Zuizui Yang

C-Wonder is an ongoing website project that will be continuously expressing aspects of human lives, such as the gradual developing of the Covid-19. What is adaption? In what way will this take us through the current world? It happened in a flash, where Covid had completely became a part of our lives and we started to live with it, no longer feel anxiety and alarm towards it. Or the adaption can be where present Covid does not exist, rather the pre-Covid life does. With different possibilities in mind, face masks became a representation to the following confusions towards the settlement of the virus to us. You can either be submerged behind the mask, and behind the Coronavirus or outside of the mask to explore the streets as if before the pandemic adapts.The website C-Wonder presents choices to the viewers through a variety of Covid life portrayal using the face masks. The moment you decide to Come scanning the QR code into the website, you are deciding to interact and char within it, just like we all did towards the pandemic. Perhaps you are wondering if there is any need to visit it at all and jump outside the mask.

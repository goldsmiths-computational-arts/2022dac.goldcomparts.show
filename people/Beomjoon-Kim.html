Beomjoon Kim

The idea of Pendulum+Digital+Fabric.pdf has started from the Chaos Theory which is even completely randomize things has a certain pattern that near future can be predicted by analyzing the preferences, repetitiveness or self-similarity. The project itself is a function of producing automatically generated digital fabric with physical pendulum to each individuals but also in a further viewpoint, looking at the installation and users in a frame, I could overlap an image of ancient Greece scholars using simple physics tools like pendulums but with the same action, it results in creating digital images made with multiple technologies combined like programming, physical computing, digital visualization, etc. This contrast but also harmony of ancient technology and modern technology is the artistic beauty I aimed for. I believe interacting with this art piece can give an experience of feeling human heritage to the audiences. Also, combining notions of art, fashion, physics, computing, I tried to show an example of how to make ideas into realization for the upcoming future. In my perspective, the development of technology will let humans to break away from limitations once more and having perspective of deconstruction is one or many viewpoint that I want to suggest to bring ideas into realization.

Technical and process details: Using Arduino with three magnetic hall sensors, the sensors collects sensor values from magnetic pendulum when interacting with users. The collected sensor values are converted throughout algorithms and generates a set of numbers which gives a private code for each of the individual users. These numbers affects the outcome of digital images as indicators of colors and patterns so that every individual users gets their own digital fabric. The coding for digital images are worked on Openframeworks, Perlin Noise and mesh were main skills I used to create. By printing out generated private code with digital fabric from an installed printer in a A4 paper, one sequence of interaction are ended and resets the system for the next person.
